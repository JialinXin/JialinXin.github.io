That is it! A work in progress. Just want to make it simpler to remember key Azure links. While I love the content on the Azure website, sometimes I have to click around a lot to get to the meat and potatoes of it all. So I’ve done that for you. Your dinner below is now served

All of my shortcuts will start with

aka.ms/Azure/ <the thing>

If you are taking Azure Certification Exams, or just want a good overview of key concepts, with easy to remember URLs, then enjoy!

Got an idea for new links, or you found broken links below? Send an email to Azure Shortcuts!
